Delicious Poha Recepi

Ingredient

  • onion
  • 2 tablespoon
  • casiew 100 gm
  • peanut 100 gm
  • kolum poha
  • salt
  • jeera
  • mustard
  • haldi

Instruction

  1. first take a one kadai
  2. add 2 table spoon of oil
  3. till the oil is heating add jeera to it
  4. and then add chopped onion int it
  5. till the onion become reddish
  6. add poha,salt,casiew in it and mix it well
